Professional profile

Technology becomes valuable when it solves a real need.

My work bridges advanced research and hands-on implementation, with a strong focus on clear communication, reliable results and knowledge transfer.

I am an Electronics and Telecommunications Engineer with a PhD in Electronics and Telecommunications. My academic work focuses on RFID systems, antenna design, microwave structures and intelligent indoor localization.

My technical practice also covers Python programming, Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, embedded development, IoT, artificial intelligence, computer vision, mobile applications, 3D design and 3D printing.

Alongside engineering and research, I teach robotics and computer science and founded Azul Academy, a learning environment dedicated to robotics, programming, STEM and innovation through practical projects.

I bring together research depth, engineering discipline and educational clarity to transform complex technical ideas into practical, understandable solutions.
